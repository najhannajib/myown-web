JPJ | MyJPJ

Project Info:

Road Transport Department (JPJ) information can be obtained through the JPJ Public Portal and social media such as Facebook and Instagram. Now searching for information on the JPJ portal can also be done through the Mobile Application, which is more easily accessible and faster. Users can also get free services such as summons review.

Project Details:

  • Client:JPJ
  • Industry:Government Agency
  • Technologies:Flutter, Laravel
  • Date:December 1, 2022
  • URL:https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.jpj.jpj_info
